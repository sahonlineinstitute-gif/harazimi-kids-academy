About the Program

Sheikh Aliyu Harazimi Kids Islamic Academy is a home learning Islamic program designed for children aged 4–6 years.

The program lasts for two years and helps children build a strong Islamic foundation through Qur'an learning, Islamic manners, and basic Arabic.

Our Courses

Qur'an Learning

Teaching children how to read the Qur'an with Tajweed.

Arabic Language

Step-by-step Arabic learning for children.

Islamic Studies

Teaching Islamic knowledge and good manners.

Home Learning Requirements

This program is a home learning program. Children will learn from home with the help of their parents.

Parents should prepare:

  • A copy of the Holy Qur'an
  • Writing notebook
  • Pen or pencil
  • Quiet study space at home
  • Parent or guardian support

Lesson Description

Our lessons are designed to make learning easy and enjoyable for young children.

  • Learning how to read the Holy Qur'an
  • Practicing Islamic manners
  • Learning basic Arabic words
  • Developing love for Islamic teachings
